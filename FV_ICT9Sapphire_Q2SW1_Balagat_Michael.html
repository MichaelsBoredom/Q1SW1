Shadow Fight 2 (MOBILE GAME)

Main Characters Description
Shadow The protagonist and the main character of the game.
This character can be controlled by players throughout the whole game and story.
He is a legendary warrior that he was driven by his arrogance to seek someone worthy matching his or her skills.
During his seek to find someone worthy, he ended up finding a gate.
The gates are known to be called as the Gates of Shadows.
When he opened the Gates of Shadows, his physical body was being consumed to the demons that they are unleashed.
As the demons seeking to return the demon seals and close the Gates of Shadows, once again, he intends to lock them away the evil he has unleashed throughout the world.
Sensei One of the main characters in the game.
Also be controlled by the player but only playable in Old Wounds, only in a exclsuive gamemode.
He is introduced and met the first time at the beginning of the game.
It is the first person to show you the ropes to play the game, like basic controls, equipment, and fighting moves.
He ends up accompanying shadow in his long journey to find the seals and close the Gates of Shadows.
May She is one of the main characters in the game.
Can't be controlled by the player due to being a main figure of the story.
She introduces herself to Shadow in Act I and Shadow would take her along on his journey.
She is an local armor and weapons expert.
She promises to make sure his equipment and armor is in good condition and an ally to him.
